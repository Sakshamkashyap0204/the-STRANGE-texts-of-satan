HISTORICAL PLACES IN HARYANA

Star Monument, Bhiwani

If you’re a history buff and planning a trip to Haryana, don’t forget to visit the Star Monument in Bhiwani. This striking landmark is a point of pride for the city and is steeped in history. The star-shaped structure is made up of 52 iron poles, each of which is adorned with ornate ironwork. Standing tall at a height of 25 feet, the monument is an impressive sight.

Qutub khan's tomb, Gurugram(Gurgaon)

Qutub Khan's Tomb is an 18th century structure built in the memory of Adam Khan who served as a general to Emperor Akbar. The tomb has a unique octagonal structure and is an illustration of Mughal architecture. In 1830, Blake, a British Officer chose to reside at the location and removed all the graves.

As one of the historical monuments of Haryana, Qutub Khan’s Tomb in Gurugram is truly a sight to behold. Rai Bal Mukund Dass (Birbal Ka Chhatta), Narnaul

Have you ever heard of Rai Bal Mukund Dass, also known as Birbal Ka Chhatta? This historical monument is located in the city of Narnaul in the Indian state of Haryana. The structure was built in the early 18th century and is a beautiful example of Mughal architecture. The intricate carvings on the walls and pillars are a testament to the skilled craftsmanship of the time.
